Vikas Khanna

Vikas Khanna, born in 1971 in Amritsar, is a Michelin-starred chef renowned for his restaurant Junoon in New York, which held a Michelin star for six consecutive years.

Interests

He is passionate about Indian cuisine, storytelling, and philanthropy.

Education

Vikas studied at the Welcomgroup Graduate School of Hotel Administration and Le Cordon Bleu in the U.S.

Famous Foods

His signature dishes include Tandoori Salmon and Lamb Shank Rogan Josh.

Additionally, he directed the Oscar-shortlisted film *The Last Color* and led the Feed India initiative.
